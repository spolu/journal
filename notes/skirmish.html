Skirmish

  • A calendar event 2-3 hours blocked by all participants
  • A leader who prepares the session by splitting the work in a set of 10-15mn tasks organized as a DAG, assigns tasks during the skirmish and answers questions from participants.
  • A branch on which developers collaborate towards the feature to ship.
  • An audio link between participants active during the entire session.
  • A warp session opened by the leader to walk developers through the code or let pair of developers coordinate their action when needed.
